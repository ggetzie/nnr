About

Welcome to No Nonsense Recipes. I made this site because I like to cook and I got tired of all the recipe websites that make you scroll past a long, irrelevant story before you get to the recipe. If it's not that, it's bombarding you with ads or begging you to sign up for a newsletter. I wanted to make a site that would be focused on the users. No Nonsense Recipes puts the user first by focusing on just the recipe, nothing else.

Because I want my incentives to be aligned with the users of the site, I charge a nominal fee to support the ongoing development and maintenance. This way I can ensure that users here are the customer, not the product. Every optimization that makes a site better at advertising, makes it worse for the users.
